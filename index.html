Projects

A showcase of some of our notable projects

Bladers
Multiplayer game

Online Multiplayer Spinning Tops is an exhilarating and realistic online multiplayer game which allows players to clash with other real-time online players in one-on-one spinning tops online multiplayer battle. Players can either play with friends or with random online players. Join this online battle and compete with 100k+ Bladers!

SAINIK
Educational AR app

The SAINIK app is made with an ambition to work as a bridge between civilians and defense personnel. The app will help society to understand and learn each and everything about the defense department which a proud and loyal citizen must know. Our Mobile Application has rich Augmented Reality-enabled features like Army Regiment Locator, Hall of fame and details of various medals, and of course Army Structure Section.

Drops
AR enabled plant watering system

Drops is a plug-and-play device which incorporates IoT in its core. We created this product with an objective to give the user the ability to not only monitor the plants remotely but also water them with the push of a button over the internet. Drops has an add-on of Augmented Reality to enhance the user experience which brings the process of watering the plant in front of the userâ€™s eyes in a fun, interactive way using 3D models and animation
