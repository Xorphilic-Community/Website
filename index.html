Hello there! XoRphilic is a student community which aims to spread and gain knowledge about the Extended Reality domain, including, but not limited to Augmented, Virtual Reality, Web XR, Game Development, 3 Dimensional modelling,etc. We are dedicated to explore and extend the possibilities of the domain to create a better tomorrow. Here, we encourage enthusiasts from all over the globe to participate, learn and walk this journey with us.

What we do

Augmented Reality

Augmented reality is an integral part of industry 4.0. Who is not fascinated by Iron Man interacting with Jarvis in reality?AR is the backbone of all these magical experiences. We specialize in making immersive AR experiences that could change the upcoming future.

Virtual Reality

Isn't it super cool to watch people wear headsets and get lost in a world that merges the line between reality and virtuality? Virtual Reality creates experiences that can blow the mind. We work with VR technolgies to create futuristic experiences.

Game development

Game development is the art of creating games involving concept generation, level design, idea, build, test and so much more. Our members work to create both 2D and 3D games that are engaging, high quality and like no other. Our games are unique and very addictive! Some of which are now available on Google Play Store.

3D Modelling

From gaming to all other extended reality innovations, 3D assets are the life and breath of them all. Without the correct designed models, all XR experiences are incomplete. We value the importance of assets of every project and give special care in building 3D models that enhance our works optimally.

PhobiVR

Project Brief:

A Virtual Reality application that creates simulations based on the chosen phobia. For example, for people having fear of heights, a virtual simulation is created that takes the user to a building having certain height and lets user perform certain activities that could help reduce their fear for heights.

NewspapAR

Project Brief:

NewspapAR is an AR application that dynamically collects latest news regarding specific headlines and displays them in real-time on a sample sheet. It has tremendous scope as it reduces the cost of buying newspaper every day and hence also saves paper. This project is on its way to be more functional in order to adept to latest features.
