About Us

XoRphilic is a student community which aims to spread and gain knowledge about the Extended Reality domain, including, but not limited to Augmented, Virtual Reality, WebXR, Game Development, 3D modeling,etc. We are dedicated to explore and extend the possibilities of the domain to create a better tomorrow.

Projects

A showcase of some of our notable projects

Bladers
Multiplayer game

Online Multiplayer Spinning Tops is an exhilarating and realistic online multiplayer game which allows players to clash with other real-time online players in one-on-one spinning tops online multiplayer battle. Join this online battle and compete with 100k+ Bladers!

SAINIK
Educational AR app

The SAINIK app is made with an ambition to work as a bridge between civilians and defense personnel. Our Mobile Application has rich Augmented Reality-enabled features like Army Regiment Locator, Hall of fame and details of various medals, and of course Army Structure Section.

Drops
AR enabled plant watering system

Drops is a plug-and-play device which incorporates IoT in its core. Drops has an add-on of Augmented Reality to enhance the user experience which brings the process of watering the plant in front of the user’s eyes in a fun, interactive way using 3D models and animation

SmartAqua

SmartAqua is an Internet of Things (IoT) initiative that automates plant watering. We all know how vital it is to water plants in order for them to live longer, yet doing so has become increasingly difficult owing to a variety of factors. So, in order to automate the procedure, we built an IoT-based project that would monitor the moisture level of the soil.
